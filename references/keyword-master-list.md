## How to Read This File

- **Vol** = estimated monthly US searches (rounded; sourced from Semrush/research cycles)

- **CPC** = cost-per-click (proxy for affiliate monetization value)
- **Status** = ✅ DONE · 🔴 NEXT · 🟡 QUEUED · ⚪ BACKLOG
- **Priority** = HIGH / MED / LOW — calculated by (Vol × (1/KD) × affiliate value)
